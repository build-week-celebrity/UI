Dead or Alive

How well do you know your Tinseltown Stiffs?

Photo by Mathew Schwartz on Unsplash

Do you and your friends find yourselves arguing about famous dead people? Well, stop arguing about weird things in public, and find out definitively who knows the most about those whom are no more, have ceased to be, and/or are bereft of life.

With this quick quiz, you can discover, beyond the shadow of a doubt, who among you has learned the most about the bereft of life, the resting in peace, those whom have kicked the bucket, shuffled off this mortal coil, and run down the curtain to join the choir invisible. Ex-people.
